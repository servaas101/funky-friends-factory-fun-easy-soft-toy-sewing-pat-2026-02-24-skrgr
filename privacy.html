Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Funky Friends Factory collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website, browse patterns, and place orders.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of the site.

Last updated: 24 February 2026

1. Introduction

Funky Friends Factory ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information about you when you visit or make a purchase from our website or otherwise interact with our services (collectively, the "Services").

This policy applies to all visitors, customers, and any other users of the Services. Please read it carefully to understand what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. If you have any questions, you can contact us using the details in the Contact Information section below.

By accessing or using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, you should stop using the Services immediately.

2. Information we collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, information that is automatically collected when you use the Services, and information from third parties. The types of information we may collect include:

2.1 Information you provide to us

When you interact with us through the Services, you may choose to provide certain personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name and contact details (such as email address and, where applicable, postal address and phone number).
  • Account details (such as login information, if we offer account functionality).
  • Order and purchase information (such as products purchased, order amounts, billing and shipping details).
  • Messages and correspondence (such as questions submitted through contact forms or via email).
  • Any other information you choose to provide when participating in surveys, promotions, or giveaways.

2.2 Information collected automatically

When you use the Services, we and our service providers may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

  • Device and browser information (such as IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers).
  • Usage data (such as pages viewed, links clicked, time spent on pages, referring/exit pages, and search terms).
  • Approximate location information derived from your IP address.
  • Log data and diagnostic information used to monitor, maintain, and improve the Services.

This information is typically collected using cookies, pixels, and similar tracking technologies. For more details, see the Cookie Policy section below.

2.3 Information from third parties

We may receive information about you from third parties, such as:

  • Payment processors and e-commerce platforms that process orders on our behalf.
  • Analytics and advertising partners that provide aggregated or anonymized information about website traffic and performance.
  • Social media platforms if you interact with our content or choose to connect your social media account to our Services.

The information we receive from third parties is governed by the privacy policies of those third parties. We use such information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, which may include:

  • Providing, operating, and maintaining the Services, including processing orders and delivering digital or physical products.
  • Communicating with you about your orders, account, questions, or support requests.
  • Customizing and improving your experience, including recommending patterns or content that may interest you.
  • Monitoring and analyzing usage, trends, and activities in connection with the Services to help us understand how they are used and where improvements are needed.
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues, security incidents, and fraudulent or illegal activity.
  • Complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our terms and policies.
  • Sending marketing communications, newsletters, or promotional materials, where permitted by law and your preferences.

We will only use your personal information where we have a lawful basis to do so, which may include your consent, the performance of a contract, compliance with a legal obligation, or our legitimate interests (such as operating and improving the Services).

4. Cookies and tracking technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and personalize the Services, analyze traffic, and understand how visitors use our website.

4.1 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences or items in your shopping cart.

4.2 Types of cookies we may use

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the basic functioning of the website (for example, to enable secure checkout or maintain your session).
  • Performance and analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the website, so we can measure and improve performance.
  • Functional cookies: Allow the website to remember your choices and preferences to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Advertising or marketing cookies: May be used by us or our partners to deliver relevant advertisements and measure their effectiveness, where applicable and permitted by law.

4.3 Your choices about cookies

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that if you choose to disable cookies, some features of the Services may not function properly.

For more information about cookies and how to manage them, you can refer to your browser’s help documentation or independent resources that explain how cookies work.

5. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with third parties in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We may share information with trusted third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, order fulfillment, analytics, email delivery, or website hosting.
  • Legal and safety: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests, or if we believe such action is necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.
  • Business transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable law.
  • With your consent: We may share information for any other purpose disclosed to you at the time of collection, where you have provided your consent.

Where we share personal information with third-party service providers, we require them to use it only as necessary to provide services to us and to protect it in accordance with applicable laws and reasonable security measures.

6. Data retention and security

We retain your personal information only for as long as is reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to provide the Services, meet legal, accounting, or reporting requirements, and resolve disputes.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you have reason to believe that your interaction with us is no longer secure (for example, if you suspect that the security of your account or personal data has been compromised), please contact us immediately using the details in the Contact Information section.

7. Your rights and choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have certain rights in relation to your personal information, which can include:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information in certain circumstances.
  • The right to object to or restrict certain processing of your information.
  • The right to withdraw consent where processing is based on your consent.

To exercise any of these rights (where available) or to update your preferences, please contact us using the information provided below. We may need to verify your identity before responding to certain requests, and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Children’s privacy

Our Services are aimed at adult sewists and are not intended to be used by children without appropriate adult supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age where parental consent is required by applicable law. If you believe that we have unintentionally collected personal information from a child, please contact us so that we can take appropriate steps to delete such information where required.

9. International users

Our Services may be accessed from countries other than the one in which our business is based. If you choose to use the Services from outside that country, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in jurisdictions that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence. We take appropriate steps to ensure that transfers of personal information are carried out in compliance with applicable data protection laws and that your information remains protected.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. When we make material changes, we will take appropriate steps to notify you, such as posting a prominent notice on our website or updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Your continued use of the Services after any changes to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of those changes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
